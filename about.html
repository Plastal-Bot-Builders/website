About Us

Plastal-Bot Builders is a non-profit organization dedicated to empowering the next generation of young leaders through Robotics, innovation, and Entrepreneurship in underrepresented communities. We believe that bridging the digital divide and fostering innovation are crucial for every community's socioeconomic progress. Our commitment to education, empowerment, and community development drives our core programs: D'Privilege, the School Outreach Program (SOP), Afrohacks.

Mission

We're dedicated to expanding access to high-quality technology education, networking, and job opportunities for marginalized communities.

Vision

Plastal-Bot Builders envisions a world where everyone, everywhere has access to life-changing technology education, mentorship, and employment opportunities.

Strategy

Our aim is to empower young adults with purpose-driven, tech-savvy skills through comprehensive training programs, mentorship, and networking opportunities in the technology sector. We prioritize creating a supportive and collaborative environment to foster innovation and creativity.

Problem

Zambia's youth face significant barriers to accessing quality education and mentorship opportunities in technology and entrepreneurship. The lack of resources, training, and support systems hinders their ability to thrive and contribute to their communities' growth.Our survey of 800 young adults aged 18-30 in communities we serve revealed staggering statistics: 81% lack internet access, 65% lack computer access, 41% lack electricity, and 80% have only primary or secondary education.

Meet Our team

Our team is comprised of passionate individuals who are committed to empowering Zambia's youth through technology and entrepreneurship. We are dedicated to providing mentorship, resources, and opportunities to help young people thrive and make a positive impact in their communities.
